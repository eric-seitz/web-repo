about me

My name is Eric Seitz, and I'm a media production student at CU Boulder with a focus on photography and sound design/music production. I'm based out of Denver, CO, but I'm always willing to travel for the right opportunity. I'm passionate about creating art that speaks to me, and I hope some of it speaks to you too! The foundation of who I am and what I do is curiosity, and I feel that through photography, cinematography, sound, writing, and design that I can help you bring any project to life in the way you've envisioned it.
